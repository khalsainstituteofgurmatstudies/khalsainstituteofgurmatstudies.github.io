Gurdwara Sahib

This Gurdwara Sahib has served the Sangat in Hamilton and surrounding areas since 1974. Registered as Gurdwara Gursikh Sangat Hamilton-Wentworth is the second oldest Gurdwara Sahib in Ontario. It was dedicated to the Martyrs of Sikh Panth in 2002, given the name among sangat, Shaheedgarh Sahib. Gurdwara Sahib is located in the beautiful green valley of Dundas with Royal Botanical Garden close by. Sangat gathers every Sunday at noon and at all major occasions. You are welcome to join.
