Shaheedgarh Sahib’s Sangat donates $10,000 for the Stem Cell Transplant Unit at Juravinski Hospital

  • by Gurdwara Sahib
  • —
  • 02 min read

CONNECT

Dear Sangat Ji, We are pleased to announce that on the day of celebrating Guru Tegh Bahadur Ji’s martyrdom, which was organized by the women of Shaheedgarh Sahib’s sangat, Gurdwara Sahib donated $10,000 to the Juravinski Hospital for the development of Stem Cell Transplant Unit for adults with the help of the sangat. All the congregation presented the cheque to Mr. K.C Carruthers, Vice President of Development at Juravinski Hospital and Cancer centre. Hamilton Spectator was also there to cover the event. We are attaching the link to the newspaper website where the event was covered. https://www.thespec.com/news-story/7965049-sikh-temple-donates-10-000-toward-stem-cell-unit/
